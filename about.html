Collateral Commons

.why

This small field research aims to enrich -and maybe contest- the dominant western narratives around AI-infused tomorrows, by collecting opinions of a wide range of individuals around these questions. We are curious about how people see artificial intelligence affecting their lives. While we assume a state of heterogeneity, diversity and complexity for our technological futures, we are questioning how these plural visions will come together to compose a creolized world.

.what

This project hatched during spring 2019 at the School of Disobedience in Berlin, by Carole, Dalia and Claire. Some results of this preliminary research will be shared online soon, anonymously and upon consent. Further developments of this project will follow. Stay tuned !
